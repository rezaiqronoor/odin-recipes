Omelet description here, just put something please gosh.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Frying Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Crack Egg.
  2. Whisk the Egg until the white and yellow part are mixed.
  3. Add Salt and whisk it again for 3 seconds.
  4. Pour into pan, medium heat.
  5. Flip after 40 seconds
  6. Take it out and serve it.
